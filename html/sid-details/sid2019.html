Safer Internet Day 2019

Venue: Abuja, Nigeria

Date: 10/02/20

Time: 10:00

On the day, a number of schools were represented by both students and teachers, as well as parents, partners and policy makers. The students were given a detailed background on Safer Internet Day (SID) and Google's aim to support the event, going forward. Dignitaries and keynote speakers went on to advice students on the importance of Online Safety.
