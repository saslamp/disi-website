Our Story

Digital Inclusion and Safer Internet (DISI) is a core program of PPDC which seeks to promote universal access to ICTs for sustained individual and economic development; and for full participation in society; in a way that preserves the safety and freedom of the internet.

DISI emerged from our realization that the internet provides a level playing field for participatory governance and economic opportunities across industries; thus the need for universal access. Recognizing the amorality of the internet, DISI also seeks to preserve internet freedom by encouraging and institutionalizing safer internet practises; through self-regulation and consideration for other people online.



Our Goal

We aim to use our programming to successfully demonstrate the need for universal access to, and use of digital technologies; so everyone could have the opportunity to fully participate in society and realize their potential. We also aim to ensure that all online users engage in a respectful way, stay safe while using the internet and enjoy a better digital experience.

Our Aims/ Objectives

Digital Inclusion and Safer Internet (DISI) is a core program of PPDC which seeks to promote universal access to ICTs for sustained individual and economic development; and for full participation in society; in a way that preserves the safety and freedom of the internet.

  • To advocate for the adoption of online safety courses into Nigerian School curriculum.
  • To build a digital village that will demonstrate/showcase the positive economic and social impact of the digital economy through citizens participation.
  • To ensure accessible and affordable internet access for the digitally excluded
  • To foster the responsible use of the internet.
  • Encourage/promote self regulation online.
  • To promote digital literacy and ICT skills acquisition for full citizens participation in Governance.
